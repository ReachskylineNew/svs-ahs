About the Faculty

Holistic Healthcare
Education at SCSVMV

A pillar of assurance in the healthcare domain — nurturing versatile professionals across a wide spectrum of allied health specialties.

Allied Health Sciences stand as a pillar of assurance within the healthcare domain, offering a diverse array of disciplines that play a pivotal role in patient care. Their expertise spans medical laboratory technology, physiotherapy, occupational therapy, optometry, dialysis technology, radiography, cardiac technology, and nutrition.

SCSVMV launched new courses under the Faculty of Allied Health Sciences for the academic year 2024–25. The courses are designed to impart quality knowledge and produce healthcare professionals who shall apply their expertise to prevent disease transmission, diagnose, treat, and rehabilitate people of all ages.

SCSVMV is situated in a safe, student-friendly, green, sprawling campus of around 50 acres at Enathur, Kanchipuram. The University provides state-of-the-art labs, international library, world-class gym, and top-notch sports facilities.

🎯

Our Vision

To be a leading faculty in allied health sciences higher education, fostering innovation, research, and ethical practice — creating healthcare professionals who serve with compassion and technical excellence.

🚀

Our Mission

To impart quality knowledge and produce skilled healthcare professionals who apply their expertise in preventing disease, diagnosing conditions, treating patients, and rehabilitating individuals of all ages and specialties.

🌟

Our Approach

One of our key strengths is a holistic approach to patient care. We offer wide-ranging career opportunities in clinical roles, research, education, and administration, empowering graduates to advocate for their patients and communities.

Courses Offered

Programs Designed for
Healthcare Careers

Choose a programme designed to launch your career in the dynamic world of healthcare and allied health sciences.

🦴
Bachelor of Physiotherapy
BPT

An in-depth program focusing on physical rehabilitation, musculoskeletal assessment, therapeutic exercises, and neuromuscular re-education to restore movement and reduce pain in patients.

4 Years + 6 Months Internship
👁️
Bachelor of Optometry
B.Optom

Trains students in the science of vision care, ocular disease management, low vision rehabilitation, and contact lens practice, equipping them to serve as primary eye care providers.

3 Years + 1 Year Internship
❤️
B.Sc Cardiac Technology
Cardiac Tech

Prepares students in cardiac diagnostics, ECG interpretation, echocardiography, treadmill testing, and invasive/non-invasive cardiac procedures essential for cardiovascular patient care.

3 Years + 1 Year Internship
📡
B.Sc Radiology & Imaging Technology
Radiology

Covers radiographic techniques, CT, MRI, ultrasound imaging, radiation safety, and image processing — training students to operate diagnostic imaging equipment in clinical settings.

3 Years + 1 Year Internship
💧
B.Sc Dialysis Technology
Dialysis Tech

A specialized programme covering renal physiology, hemodialysis, peritoneal dialysis, blood access care, and water treatment systems for patients with chronic kidney disease.

3 Years + 1 Year Internship
